Common questions

Straight answers about studying on Infotris and what we offer today.

Do I need prior experience to start?

No. Paths like Frontend Developer and Python Developer start from foundations. If you already code, jump to intermediate courses via the course catalog sidebar.

How long should I study each path?

It depends on your pace and how much time you have. Most paths suggest roughly 6–16 weeks of focused study. Consistency matters more than speed — even a few hours a week adds up.

Is Infotris free?

Yes — you can explore all structured trails and courses at no cost. We believe access to structured learning should not depend on your budget.

How is this different from YouTube tutorials?

Tutorials teach random topics. Infotris gives you ordered trails — what to study first, what comes next, and how lessons connect toward a career-shaped goal.

Does Infotris help me get a job?

Infotris is for learning — courses, trails, and practice. We do not list jobs or handle applications. This page helps you choose what to study toward the kind of work you are interested in.

What does the Careers page mean then?

It shows learning trails inspired by common tech roles — so you know which courses to take if you are learning toward frontend work, Python, full-stack, and similar goals.

Ready to start studying?

Pick a trail, open the courses, and learn at your own pace. That is what Infotris is for today.
