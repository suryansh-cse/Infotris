Arrays and lists in depth

In Python, lists are dynamic arrays. Index access is O(1). Append is amortized O(1). Insert at the front is O(n) because items shift.

nums = [3, 1, 4, 1, 5]
nums.append(9)      # fast at end
nums.insert(0, 0)   # slow at front
